Planet Clicker 2 is a highly engaging, space-themed idle clicker game where you build a cosmic empire by generating resources, upgrading technologies, and expanding across the solar system. As the sequel to the popular Planet Clicker, this installment introduces more planets, deeper strategic elements, and enhanced visuals for an even more addictive experience.

How to Play

Click on planets to generate energy and resources. Use your accumulated energy to purchase upgrades that boost production and unlock automation. Navigate between planets to discover new worlds, each with unique bonuses and technologies. Your goal is to expand from humble beginnings on Earth to mastering the entire solar system.

Controls

  • Mouse Click — Click on planets to generate energy
  • Click Upgrade Icons — Purchase improvements with accumulated energy
  • Mouse Wheel / Arrow Keys — Navigate between planets and menus

Launched in January 2018, it has received updates adding new planets, features, and gameplay depth. Its popularity stems from the satisfying progression loop and the thrill of expanding from Earth to an interstellar civilization.
